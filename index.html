Call for Lightning Talks

We invite short lightning talks from students and researchers across New England on any topic in computer systems. Talks are 5-20 minutes and may present early ideas, ongoing projects, or recently published work.

Submit a title and abstract. Slides are optional.

Topics of Interest

Operating systems, Distributed systems, File and storage systems, System security, Machine learning systems, Big data systems, Embedded systems, Networking, Computer architecture, Programming languages, and Other topics in computer systems.
